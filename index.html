Handwoven Bike Basket

Orange block, tiered shape · The Basket Room

A two-tone basket with a vibrant band of orange, woven from veta vera grass — the same elephant grass that grows wild across Ghana. Each one takes up to three days to weave by hand.

It comes with two leather straps and gold-toned buckles, ready for the front of your bike. Perfect for picnics, paperback novels, market peaches, and bunches of flowers exactly like the ones in the picture.
